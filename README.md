## About This Repository

This repository documents my cybersecurity knowledge, learning, and applied
security work developed across my academic studies and professional experience.

During my BSc programme I completed two dedicated security modules:

- **Security** — Pearson BTEC Level 5 HND in Computing (Software Engineering)
- **Advanced Cyber Security (CET324)** — BSc (Hons) Computer Systems
  Engineering Final Year — University of Sunderland

In addition to academic study, I completed a six month IT Systems Administrator
internship at Addon Engineering Solutions, Kathmandu where I gained hands-on
experience in network security, access control management, system monitoring,
and infrastructure protection.

My motivation for specialising in cybersecurity is both professional and
deeply personal. During my internship period, my wife became the victim of
an online financial scam. Witnessing that experience — and feeling the gap
between the technical knowledge I possessed and the protection I could
actually provide — made my professional direction permanently clear.

Nepal's digital economy is expanding rapidly while cybersecurity awareness
and infrastructure remain critically underdeveloped. Millions of ordinary
people engage with digital financial services for the first time without
the knowledge or tools to protect themselves. I am committed to building
the advanced expertise needed to contribute meaningfully to digital safety
in Nepal — as a practitioner, consultant, and advocate for cybersecurity
awareness at the community level.

I am currently preparing for the Master of Information Technology
at the University of Waikato, New Zealand, with a focus on Cybersecurity.
